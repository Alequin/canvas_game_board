Canvas Boards

Falling Squares

Coloured squares randomly generate at the top of the grid and move slowly down. When they reach the bottom they are removed and new squares are created at the top (if the square limit is not reached).

Movement Square

A single square on the canvas moves around, selection a random direction on each movement.

User Movement

Gives the user control of the square movement. The arrow keys are used for up, right, left and down movements. Focus must be on the canvas or the square will note move (if nothing is moving click on the canvas to give it focus).
